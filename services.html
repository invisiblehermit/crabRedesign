services


Cancer Research And Biostatistics (CRAB) began in 1997 and has since grown to a staff of 90 clinical research professionals located in our Seattle, Washington headquarters. We continue to strengthen our core offerings of biostatistical leadership, data management expertise, and electronic data capture solutions, while expanding our services to meet the needs of our clients. As a leader in the field of oncology, we are pleased that biopharmaceutical companies, research alliances, cancer consortia, and oncology-focused cooperative groups continue to turn to CRAB as their partner for oncology clinical drug development.
We remain focused on our mission by combining our oncology experience, expertise, and innovation in oncology clinical trial management to advance treatments and help conquer cancer. As such, our goal is to deliver for our collaborators, key partners and clients: Trusted, high quality expertise, on time and on budget. We think our track record and reputation speaks for itself.
