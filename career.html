Conquering cancer takes cutting edge science, innovative approaches, effective systems, and the latest tools. In helping in the fight against cancer, CRAB credits our success to hiring and developing talented, committed people who use innovative data management methods and applications of biostatistics that are central to our research. In our hiring process we strive to find employees who fit within our culture and are motivated to deliver outstanding results. CRAB is committed to maintaining a professional and rewarding work environment to support each employee’s work-life balance. We offer a collaborative, stimulating and inclusive work environment. We provide an excellent benefit package which includes medical, dental, vision, 403(b) retirement contribution, life & LTD insurance, generous paid time off and much more! CRAB is an equal opportunity employer with a commitment to diversity. Thank you for your interest in CRAB.
